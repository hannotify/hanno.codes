Meetup with Dylan Beattie: Open-Source, Open Mind

Why do people give away their code for free? What motivates millions of developers worldwide to dedicate their time to open-source projects?

Find out the answers to these questions during the talk “Open-Source, Open Mind: The Cost of Free Software” by Microsoft MVP Dylan Beattie – a major authority on all things open-source.

Event details

Date: Wednesday, March 19, 2025

Time:
Doors open: 17:30
Dinner: 18:00 – 19:00 (free of charge)
Meetup: 19:00 – 20:15

Locatie: Info Support, Seminar Room 1, Kruisboog 42, 3905TG, Veenendaal

About the talk

Dylan will take you through the history, present, and future of open-source software. He’ll discuss the origins of the open-source movement, licenses and legalities and some of the weird and wonderful edge cases that free software has created over the years. Moreover he’ll answer the question: “is a truly sustainable open source ecosystem possible, and if so - what might it look like?”

Do you love open-source, just like us? Then you won’t want to miss this meetup!
